Well… when I was in college it was honestly so annoying trying to keep track of where my money was going or which spots even took my campus funds. I legit had to check YikYak half the time just to figure it out. So I was like, “why not just make something that actually helps with this?” Plus, I was kinda trash at managing my own spending, so lowkey I built this to help myself too 😅

Only the basics like your email, username, and what you log. Just so your stuff saves when you log in. That’s it.
