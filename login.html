🌿 Nooksii Terms of Service

Updated: August 5, 2025

Hey! Welcome to Nooksii 💛

This app was made with one goal: to help students keep track of their campus spending in a simple, cozy, stress free way. We’re not here to profit off your info, and we really don’t care about collecting data on you. Promise.

🧠 What is Nooksii?

Nooksii is a little app built by students, for students. It helps you:

  • track how much money you’ve spent (or have left) on campus
  • log little purchases like coffees and snacks
  • see your spending habits and plan better

That’s it! No ads, no selling stuff, no hidden charges. Just helpful vibes.

🔐 What Info We Collect (And What We Don’t)

We keep it minimal:

  • your email and username, so your account can exist
  • your spending balance and logged items, so your info shows up when you log in

What we don’t do:

  • ❌ we don’t sell your data
  • ❌ we don’t track your behavior
  • ❌ we don’t care about your habits beyond helping you
  • ❌ we’re not sending anything to 3rd parties, advertisers, or any creepy data people

Your data stays between you and Nooksii, and honestly… we don’t even wanna look at it unless we have to fix a bug or help you out.

💫 Deleting Your Account

Don’t wanna use Nooksii anymore? That’s totally okay. You can delete your account any time in the dashboard. It will remove everything related to you from our system. No drama, no weird “please stay!” popups. Just poof... it’s gone.

🚫 What Not to Do

We’re a small team (like… one person lol :3), so we ask that you please:

  • don’t try to break or hack the site
  • don’t spam or troll
  • don’t pretend to be someone else
  • don’t use it for anything other than good, helpful reasons
  • don't use anything NSFW or else you will be banned forever (no appeals)

Basically: be a good human and respect the space 🌱

⚠️ No Promises, Just Vibes

This is a personal project. It’s not backed by a big company. That means:

  • things might go down sometimes
  • bugs can happen
  • we can’t guarantee perfection
  • we’re not liable if something glitches or your balance looks off

That said, I do our best to keep it stable and helpful. If something breaks, and I'll try to fix it fast.

📌 Updates to These Terms

If we ever change how Nooksii works or update our policies, we’ll let you know ahead of time. Nothing shady, no surprise changes. You’ll always be in the loop.
